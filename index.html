Ha Long Bay

About Ha Long Bay

Located in northeast Vietnam, Ha Long Bay features thousands of limestone karsts and isles in various shapes and sizes, creating one of the world's most spectacular seascapes.

  • Over 1,600 limestone islands and islets
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site since 1994
  • Name means "descending dragon" in Vietnamese
  • Estimated to be 500 million years old
